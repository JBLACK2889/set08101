What is this quiz about?

Horror movies are a cultural phenomenon.
They have been gracing our screens for decades, with horror stories spanning centuries.

Horror movies over the past fifty years have created a sort of modern day mythology.
With many of its scariest faces being known to millions across the world, such as Michael Myers, Jason and Freddy Krueger.
This quiz seeks to pay homage to these characters and their history.
